**附：** 对于一组数据 $(u_1, v_1)$，$(u_2, v_2)$，$\cdots$，$(u_n, v_n)$，其回归直线方程 $\hat{v} = \hat{\alpha} + \hat{\beta}u$ 的斜率和截距的最小二乘估计分别为：

$$\hat{\beta} = \frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n}(u_i - \bar{u})(v_i - \bar{v})}{\sum_{i=1}^{n}(u_i - \bar{u})^2}, \quad \hat{\alpha} = \bar{v} - \hat{\beta}\bar{u}$$

**决定系数：**
$$R^2 = 1 - \frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n}(v_i - \hat{v}_i)^2}{\sum_{i=1}^{n}(v_i - \bar{v})^2}$$

$$
y = c \cdot 2^{bx}\\
log_2 y = log_2 c + log_2 2^{bx}\\

\therefore \hat{b} = b , \hat{a} = log_2 \cdot c\\
代入得\\
\hat{b} = \frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n}(u_i - \bar{u})(v_i - \bar{v})}{\sum_{i=1}^{n}(u_i - \bar{u})^2} = \frac{\Sigma^6_{i = 1}(x_i - \overline{x})(z_i - \overline{z})}{\Sigma^6_{i = 1}(x_i - \overline{x})^2} = \frac{34.7}{70} \approx 0.5\\
\hat{a} = \overline{z} - \hat{b} \overline{x} = log_2 \cdot c \\
